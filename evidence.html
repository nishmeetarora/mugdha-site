Health Inc.

The book that indicts
a system and liberates
a reader.

Health Inc. is not a wellness book. It is an intellectual argument — a forensic examination of a healthcare system that profits from keeping human beings sick, confused and dependent. Written from nine years at the clinical frontier, it maps the gap between what medicine knows and what medicine does.

It is also an invitation. To remember that the body is not broken — it is speaking. That chronic illness is not a life sentence — it is a message. That what we call disease is, in most cases, the predictable result of a species that has forgotten what it is.
